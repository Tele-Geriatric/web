Our pioneering fellowship program combines cutting-edge telemedicine technology with specialized geriatric care to address the unique healthcare needs of aging populations.

Program Structure

A comprehensive 12-month fellowship designed to develop expertise in tele-geriatric care and research.

1

Months 1-4: Foundation

  • • Geriatric medicine fundamentals
  • • Telehealth platform training
  • • Digital health literacy
  • • Patient communication skills
  • • Research methodology basics
2

Months 5-8: Application

  • • Clinical rotations via telehealth
  • • Remote patient monitoring
  • • Research project initiation
  • • Quality improvement initiatives
  • • Interdisciplinary team collaboration
3

Months 9-12: Mastery

  • • Independent telehealth practice
  • • Research project completion
  • • Conference presentations
  • • Publication preparation
  • • Career transition planning

Research Focus Areas

Our fellows contribute to groundbreaking research in tele-geriatric care across multiple domains.

Remote Monitoring

Developing innovative approaches to continuous health monitoring for seniors using wearable devices and IoT technology.

Digital Health Literacy

Researching effective methods to improve technology adoption and digital health literacy among older adults.

Mental Health Support

Investigating telehealth interventions for depression, anxiety, and cognitive health in geriatric populations.

AI-Assisted Diagnosis

Exploring artificial intelligence applications in remote geriatric assessment and early disease detection.

Care Coordination

Optimizing telehealth workflows and care team coordination for complex geriatric patients.

Health Equity

Addressing disparities in telehealth access and outcomes among diverse senior populations.
