Tele‑Geriatric Research Fellowship

A one‑year, part‑time program blending clinical problems, human‑centered design, and data science to improve care for older adults via telehealth.

12.6% of Medicare beneficiaries used telehealth in Q4‑2023.

Fellowship Tracks

Clinical Innovation

Design & test tele‑neurology workflows (falls, mobility, polypharmacy) with older adults & caregivers.

Data & AI

Analyze remote‑monitoring data, build simple risk scores, and evaluate usability & outcomes.

Equity & Access

Create low‑bandwidth experiences, digital‑literacy supports, and rural‑ready toolkits.

What Fellows Do

Research Sprint

Define a geriatric neurology problem, review evidence, and co‑design a pilot with a clinic partner.

Build & Test

Prototype low‑friction tele‑tools, recruit 10–30 participants, and measure feasibility & acceptability.

Disseminate

Preprint, poster, or short paper; share toolkits for rural sites and community clinics.

Eligibility & Support (mock)

Who can apply

Clinicians, trainees, engineers, and community partners with interest in aging & telehealth. Remote‑friendly.

Time & funding

~4–6 hrs/week for 12 months. Mini‑grants up to $5k for pilots; travel micro‑grants for rural sites.

Deliverables

1 pilot, 1 dissemination product, and an equity checklist tailored for low‑bandwidth settings.
