Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

The Tele-Geriatric Research Fellowship ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you apply for the fellowship, register for events, or contact us, including:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Professional credentials and employment history
  • Academic records and research interests
  • Demographic information

Derivative Data

Information our servers automatically collect when you access the website, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and pages viewed.

Use of Your Information

We may use information collected about you to:

  • Process fellowship applications
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Improve our website and fellowship program
  • Send administrative information and program updates
  • Conduct research and analysis
  • Prevent fraudulent activities and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations:

  • With Review Committees: Your application materials will be shared with our selection committee members.
  • With Academic Partners: For fellows who participate in joint programs with our partner institutions.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default, but you can typically modify your browser settings to decline cookies.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites that are not affiliated with us. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

Security of Your Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Policy for Children

We do not knowingly solicit information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be indicated by a new "Last Updated" date.
